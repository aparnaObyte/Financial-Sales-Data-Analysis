## 📊 Project Overview
This repository contains a comprehensive, multi-page **Power BI Dashboard** that transforms raw transactional sales data into actionable business insights. The analysis tracks **$118.73M in Total Revenue** and **$16.89M in Total Profit** across global markets, optimizing visibility into product performance, regional growth, and profit margins.

The dashboard is designed with an executive dark theme layout, ensuring clear visual hierarchy and an intuitive user experience for corporate stakeholders and business analysts.

---

## 🚀 Special & Advanced Features

### 1. Multi-Page Seamless Navigation
Instead of cluttered single-view reports, this project splits the financial story into two dedicated strategic views, accessible via an interactive sidebar navigation menu:
* **Sales Analysis Dashboard:** Focuses on macro-level volume, identifying revenue engines, regional sales distributions, and timeline trend scales.
* **Profit Analysis Dashboard:** Focuses strictly on financial health, breaking down margins, cost-impact ratios, and identifying high-performing versus underperforming segments.

### 2. Dynamic Cross-Filtering & Interactive Slicers
To give executives the power to slice data dynamically, advanced filtering controls are integrated natively into the canvas:
* **Year Slicer (`2013` vs. `2014`):** Instantly filters historical performance to evaluate year-over-year operational growth.
* **Segment Multi-Select Slicer:** Toggles views across key business verticals: *Government, Small Business, Enterprise, Midmarket, and Channel Partners*.

### 3. Advanced Visual Selection & KPI Card Layouts
* **Waterfall Chart Integration:** Utilized for *Profit Margin % by Product* to showcase cumulative margins clearly, highlighting how individual products contribute step-by-step to the total profit scale.
* **Donut Chart Percentage Slices:** Represents *Profit by Segment* visually paired with clean text callouts showing exact dollar margins alongside contribution percentages (e.g., Government holding a dominant **65.04%** of profit share).
* **High-Contrast Minimalist Gauge/KPI Blocks:** Positioned at the top of the canvas to establish instantaneous baseline awareness for *Total Revenue, Total Profit, Units Sold,* and *Sales Growth %*.

## 📈 Key Metrics & Data Insights
Based on the dashboard analysis:
* **Top Revenue Product:** *Paseo* leads global sales significantly with **$33M**, followed by *VTT* at **$21M**.
* **Top Profit Engine:** *Paseo* also drives the highest net profit at **$4.8M**, while *Carretera* lags at **$1.8M**.
* **Highest Profit Margin:** *Amarilla* commands the highest individual product profit margin at **16%**, followed closely by *VTT* and *Paseo* at **15%**.
* **Dominant Market Segment:** The *Government* sector is the primary driver of profitability, contributing **$11.39M (65.04%)** to the total bottom line.
* **Geographic Performance:** Sales are highly balanced globally, led by the *United States* and *Canada* at **$27M** each, followed by *France ($26M)*, *Germany ($25M)*, and *Mexico ($23M)*.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Skills Demonstrated
* **Data Visualization Tool:** Microsoft Power BI Desktop
* **Data Transformation:** Power Query (ETL process, data cleaning, type conversion)
* **Modeling & Calculations:** DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) for custom KPI measures, time intelligence, and margin calculations
* **UI/UX Design:** Custom Canvas Grid Layout, Executive Dark Mode Palette, Synchronized Slicers, and Custom Page Navigation
